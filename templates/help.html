Help & Usage Guide

LAPS (Line Allocation & Planning System) is a decision-support tool for daily manpower planning.

What LAPS Does
  • Converts daily production plan into required manpower
  • Allocates activities to operators sequentially
  • Highlights overload and underutilization
  • Supports print and Excel export for shopfloor use
